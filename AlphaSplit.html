GLASS + AE COMBO

Another thing you can do in blender with this method is plug the alpha into the factor value of the "mix color" node.
this separates the two colors in the mix node by the alpha layer, allowing for more customization and creativivty

In this example I used a copy of the model and turned it into a glass overlay by taking just the alpha, from the image, plus 2 colors

Here's a screenshot of the blend file, showing the node setup and result

AlphaSplit + volume glow

You can also split the alpha, and use one of the 2 color attributes in the mix color node to set transparency of either the alpha or other (reverse select),
In this case I took the default cube and took some image, setting up my selection for the alpha, split the alpha (like above), than used the transparent shader to make the
white-space, finally duplicating the cube and using a volume scatter to somewhat fill the inside of the cube.
