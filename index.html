Alpha emission:

Alpha Emission is a technique that uses an image's Alpha (transparency) channel to overlay effects.
in this case the alpha channel is used to emit light

tap or click an image to toggle alpha emission effect.

GLASS + AE COMBO

Another thing you can do in blender with this method is plug the alpha into the factor value of the "mix color" node.
this separates the two colors in the mix node by the alpha layer, allowing for more customization and creativivty

In this example I used a copy of the model and turned it into a glass overlay by taking just the alpha, from the image, plus 2 colors
